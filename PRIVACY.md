# Privacy Policy

Leaguewise does not collect, store, or transmit your data. The extension runs entirely
in your browser. It reads your existing ESPN session cookies solely to request your own
league's data from ESPN's fantasy API, exactly as the ESPN website itself does. That
data, and everything computed from it, stays on your device. Leaguewise operates no
servers and includes no analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind.

Questions: admin@leaguewise.net
